张译中

Microsoft Research Asia

 

I am an associate researcher at Microsoft Research Asia. I received my Ph.D. in computer science 2016 (supervised by Prof. Kun Zhou) and B.S. in mechanical engineering 2010, both from Zhejiang University. My research interests include computational fabrication, physically based simulation, 3D reconstruction and robotics.
